About Me

My name is Casey Clair, and I am an aspiring web developer. I would describe myself as pretty laid back and easy to get along with. You will find me outdoors most of the time when I am not working. I graduated with a marketing degree from Oklahoma State University in 2015, but I felt like something was missing. I needed that extra skill that set me apart from the status quo and gave me a chance to pursue something more specialized, so I decided to give web development a try. The web has always interested me and I was eager to learn how to create amazing things and challenge myself every day. So far I feel that I have made the right career choice and I am looking forward to what the future brings.
